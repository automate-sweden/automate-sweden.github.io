**Making advanced automation practical for Swedish industry.**

Automation is becoming unavoidable ‚Äî but for many companies it is still too complex, too risky, or too fragmented to adopt at scale.  
At the same time, global competitors are accelerating rapidly by combining robotics, AI, and digital production systems in ways that reshape entire value chains.

**Automate Sweden exists to close this gap.**  
We connect industry, researchers, integrators, and technology providers to turn advanced automation from isolated pilots into scalable, deployable solutions for Swedish industry.

---

## What Automate Sweden is

Automate Sweden is a **national, federated ecosystem for industrial automation** ‚Äî and a **platform that provides shared infrastructure, coordination, and continuity**.

We are built to **close the loop between all key stakeholders** involved in automation: industry, researchers, system integrators, technology providers, and public actors.  
Instead of fragmented initiatives or one-off pilots, Automate Sweden connects needs, knowledge, infrastructure, and deployment into a continuous system.

Our focus is not automation as a standalone technology, but **automation as a shared industrial capability** ‚Äî developed collaboratively and made deployable across sectors, regions, and company sizes.

## How Automate Sweden works  
**A platform that enables an ecosystem ‚Äî and an ecosystem that feeds the platform**

At the core of Automate Sweden is a continuous loop:

**Industrial needs** ‚Üí shape **research and pilots** ‚Üí validated in **testbeds** ‚Üí translated by **integrators/providers** ‚Üí deployed in **industry** ‚Üí producing **feedback** that informs the next cycle.

This loop replaces fragmented pilots with a **repeatable pathway from idea to industrial impact**.

As a **platform**, Automate Sweden provides shared structure and continuity:
- shared test and demonstration environments  
- coordinated pilot and validation activities  
- common practices and scalable approaches  
- long-term continuity beyond individual projects

As a **federated ecosystem**, Automate Sweden enables collaboration without centralisation:
participants remain independent, but work together through shared purpose and shared infrastructure.

## Why now

Industrial automation is entering a new phase. Advances in robotics, AI, and digital production systems are accelerating ‚Äî while regulation, demographic pressure, and global competition raise the stakes.

For Sweden, this creates a narrow window of opportunity:
- to turn strong research and industrial know-how into deployable systems  
- to ensure SMEs are not left behind in the next automation wave  
- to strengthen value chains, not just individual firms  
- to shape how advanced automation is developed, deployed, and governed  

Automate Sweden is a response to this moment.

## Shaping the cluster together

Automate Sweden is being built for the long term ‚Äî but not behind closed doors.

While the core mission and structure are clear, the cluster is **intentionally under development**. The concrete focus areas, testbeds, modes of collaboration, and priorities will be shaped together with the organisations that engage.

This is a deliberate choice: advanced automation cannot be designed top-down. It must reflect real industrial needs, constraints, and opportunities ‚Äî across company sizes and sectors.
